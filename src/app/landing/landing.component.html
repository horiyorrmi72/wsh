Who we are

Women Safe House Sustenance Initiative is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting and rehabilitating women and girls from all forms of Gender Based Violence in Nigeria. We provide shelter, legal aid, healthcare and comprehensive services to women and girls of all ages who are survivors of Rape, Domestic Violence, Female Genital Mutilation, Child Marriage and Sex Trafficking. Since 2016, we have created safe spaces for several women and girls to thrive.
