women safe house

Women Safe House Sustenance Initiative is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting and rehabilitating women and girls from all forms of gender-based violence (GBV) in Nigeria. We provide shelter, legal aid, healthcare and comprehensive services to women and girls of all ages who are survivors of rape and other forms of sexual violence, domestic violence, female-genital-mutilation, child-marriage, harmful traditional practices and sex-trafficking. Since 2016, we have created safe spaces for several women and girls to thrive.
