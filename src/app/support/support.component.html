Supporting a Survivor

Survivors of abuse need understanding, compassion, and resources to heal. Hereâ€™s how you can offer support effectively.

Listen Without Judgment

Let the survivor share their story at their own pace. Avoid blaming or questioning their decisions.

Believe Them

Believe their experience and affirm their courage in sharing it. Validation is a critical step in their healing.

Encourage Professional Help

Suggest connecting with counselors, support groups, or organizations that specialize in helping survivors.

Respect Their Privacy

Keep their story confidential unless they explicitly ask you to share it for their benefit.

Offer Practical Support

Help with day-to-day needs like transportation, childcare, or finding a safe space, if they ask for it.

Stay Patient

Healing takes time. Be patient and continue to offer support without pressuring them.
