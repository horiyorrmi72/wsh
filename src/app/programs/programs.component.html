Our Programs

Empowering women in communities, one program at a time.

Explore Our Initiatives

Shelter:

Women Safe House offers emergency and temporary shelter at an undisclosed location for women and girls fleeing gender-based violence. The emergency shelter provides a trauma-informed supportive environment focused on healing. Survivors can access care coordination, counseling, healthcare, and safety planning.

Legal Services and Litigation:

Women Safe House provides legal services to women and girls experiencing gender-based violence, stalking, and human or sex trafficking in Nigeria. This service offers free legal advice, representation, and support to ensure justice for survivors.

Psychosocial services:

We believe that psychosocial services which can be instrumental in helping a survivor overcome the trauma experienced in an abusive relationship or violence faced to move forward. Psychosocial services include counseling, support groups and storytelling events which available for survivors of all forms of gender-based violence, sexual assault, stalking, or domestic abuse, whether it occurred recently or in the past. Our programs include SISTERS CIRCLE (Support Group), SAFE SISTERS (teenage girls support group) and FEARLESS (storytelling and trauma recovery retreat). Sessions are offered to women and girls as individuals or as a group to deliver emotional and psychological support to help survivors heal.

Training of Activists/Advocates on GBV:

Recognizing the importance of enhancing the skills, knowledge, and resources of activists to effectively advocate for gender equality and justice, empowering them to lead impactful campaigns, influence policies, and create sustainable change in their communities.

24-Hour Emergency Helpline:

Providing 24/7 support and referrals for survivors in crisis, ensuring they have immediate access to emotional assistance, practical resources, and connections to essential services to address their needs and promote recovery.

SAFE Community Engagement:

Engaging in community education, awareness creation, and mobilization efforts aimed at preventing gender-based violence, addressing its root causes, and equipping individuals and groups with the knowledge and tools needed to respond effectively to incidents of violence.

Employment Support for Survivors:

Supporting survivors by connecting them to job opportunities and providing skill development programs, empowering them to achieve financial independence and rebuild their lives with confidence and self-sufficiency.

Medical Services:

We ensure survivors have access to comprehensive medical care, which includes timely treatment for injuries or health conditions resulting from their experiences and appropriate referrals to specialized healthcare providers and services to address their physical and mental health needs.

Research and Documentation:

We carry out research and studies to gather data and insights that guide the development of effective interventions, while also systematically documenting cases of gender-based violence to understand better its patterns, causes, and impact on individuals and communities.

Policy Advocacy:

By advocating for and driving changes in policies and legislation to safeguard the rights of women and girls, ensuring their protection against discrimination, violence, and other forms of injustice.

Internship and Mentoring for Students/Graduates:

By providing resources, training, and mentorship to support career growth in advocacy and social work, empowering individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to drive meaningful change, influence policy, and effectively address social issues.
